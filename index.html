Welcome!

We're a group of friendly folks in the Irish Tech Community that hangout at irishtechcommunity.slack.com.

We are always interested in new members joining. There are no requirements, other than being a real person, and ideally, interested in tech. We have many channels devoted to multiple subjects, from design and development of tech, to conferences and diversity. We are building a sustainable, diverse community of people who are interested in growing their skills, experience, community and industry.

To join, just click here and we'll send you an invite link.

You must agree to our Code of Conduct before you join.

This community supports a wide range of conversations. A list of channels will give some idea of how wide that range can be.

If you're interested in your own Slack instance, please consider using our referral code!
